Our Services
At Gate & Fence Solutions, we provide a broad range of ironwork, including installation, repair, welding, and access controls, whether they are residential or commercial.
🚪

Custom Gates

Driveway gates, pedestrian gates, sliding gates, and swing gates. Every design custom-fabricated to match your property's style and security needs.

🧱

Fences & Enclosures

Wrought iron fencing, privacy screens, louver panels, and pool enclosures. Built to code with premium materials that resist LA weather.

🏪

Railings & Stairs

Interior and exterior stair railings, balcony railings, and handrails. Custom designs from modern minimalist to ornamental wrought iron.

🔑

Access Control Systems

DoorBird intercoms, keypad entry, remote access, and smart lock integration. Full security solutions for residential and commercial properties.

🔥

Welding & Repair

On-site welding, structural repairs, gate motor replacement, and restoration of existing ironwork. Fast response for emergency repairs.

⚙

Custom Ironwork

Pergolas, window guards, decorative panels, louver screens, and architectural metalwork. If you can dream it, we can build it.
