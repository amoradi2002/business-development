Our Services

🚪

Custom Gates

Driveway gates, pedestrian gates, sliding gates, and swing gates. Every design custom-fabricated to match your property's style and security needs.

🧱

Fences & Enclosures

Wrought iron fencing, privacy screens, louver panels, and pool enclosures. Built to code with premium materials that resist LA weather.

🏪

Railings & Stairs

Interior and exterior stair railings, balcony railings, and handrails. Custom designs from modern minimalist to ornamental wrought iron.

Access Control Systems

Our Story

Family-Owned. Built on Trust.

Gate & Fence Solutions Inc. is a family-owned ironwork company based in Los Angeles. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, our team has built a reputation for quality craftsmanship, honest pricing, and reliable service across LA County.

We started in a small shop with a simple idea: build things right the first time. Today, we've completed over 500 projects — from custom driveway gates in Beverly Hills to commercial security fencing in Downtown LA. Every project is designed, fabricated, and installed by our own crew.

Whether you need a specific type of gate or fence or have unique design requirements, our team of experts is here to assist you. We specialize in creating unique projects and custom ironwork, such as fences, gates, railings, pergolas, and access control systems.
